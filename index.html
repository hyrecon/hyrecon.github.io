About

I am a research assistant in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Pusan National University (B.S. '26), advised by Prof. Sangkil Kim.

Outside the lab, I swim, lift weights, and spend much time taking things apart just to see how they work. I've taught Arduino and 3D printing to students in rural Thailand, and I still believe the best ideas come from getting your hands dirty.

I am broadly interested in the following areas:

Backscatter Communications Channel Modeling & Estimation RF/Antenna Design Software-Defined Radio (SDR) AI/DL for Wireless communications Wireless Sensing IoT Systems
